Avabar

Avabar

Avabar is a social iphone application for the bar scene. I like to think of it as a Groupon meets Match.com meets the bar scene type of app. It allows users to create accounts and profiles, matches users to other users based on preferences, and find deals and bars in their area. Written complete in Objective C with a php backend. There is also a matching Android application.

  • Client: Avabar
  • Tools: Objective-C, Xcode
  • Year: 2013
